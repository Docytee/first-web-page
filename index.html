Welcome to Docytee Research Group.

Docytee Research Group is a non-governmental organization in Ejigbo, Osun State, Nigeria. It was established on 14th June, 2002. The objective for its establishment is to give effect to the world's drive for the much-needed research in science, engineering and especially technology.

Our Vision.

To become a world class and world's leading academic research website, recognized for its excellence in transformative discoveries and innovation.

Our Mission.

The mission of Docytee Research Group is to advance world-class research and innovation across the entire world. We provide the word with comprehensive, solution-oriented servoce and infrastructure to support research, dissemination and translation of discoveries to minimize impact.
